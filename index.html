A bit about Bear Grylls

Bear Grylls was born on the 7th of June in 1974. He came from a family of cricketers and grew up in Northern Ireland until the age of 4.
He first became popular from the series "Man vs. Wild" where he dropped into inhospitable
places and showed people how to survive. Some stunts included climbing cliffs,
parachuting from helicopters, running through forest fires, eating snakes,
wrestling alligators and many more crazy things.
